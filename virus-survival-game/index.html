🕹️ Virus Survival Game

How to Play & Game Legend

  • 🧍 Reach the 🏁 exit safely.
  • 💣 Bomb / ☠️ Virus = Game Over.
  • ⚠ Trap sends you back to start.
  • 🛡 Shield protects you for 3 turns.
  • ⚡ Double Move lets you move twice.
  • ⭐ Energy gives extra points.
  • 👾 Watch out for the Enemy – getting caught ends the game.
  • Use W / A / S / D or Arrow Keys on desktop.
  • Use Swipe or Buttons on mobile.
